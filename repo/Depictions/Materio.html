Materio
Materio is a Material Design inspired theme for GroovyLock. Even though it is just a simple clock, it looks absolutely stunning on any device.
With it's stretching and compressing bars, it will leave you staring at your lockscreen all day.

Weather information may be added later, as well as some configurations such as a dark mode for lighter wallpapers.

Instructions for manually enabling the dark mode:
Included in this package in the css folder is a stylesheet named style-dark.css. If you would like to apply this, change the line
<link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="css/style-light.css" />
to
<link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="css/style-dark.css" /> and Materio should now have the dark theme applied.
If it is not, try disabling and enabling GroovyLock, or respring your device.
